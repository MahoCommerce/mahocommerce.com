We're thrilled to announce that Maho will be making its first-ever live appearance at the upcoming MagentiamoDay
2024 in Milan, Italy.

<div style="clear: both"></div>

<!-- more -->

MagentiamoDay is the Italian Magento community conference, it's a day filled with insightful presentations,
networking opportunities, and the latest innovations in e-commerce technology.

![Fabrizio Balliano at MagentiamoDay 2024](/assets/blog/magentiamoday2024.jpg){ loading=lazy }

Fabrizio will be presenting Maho to the audience with a speach about how the 15+ year long history of the 
open source M1 platform allowed this amazing new platform to be born and will walk through all the features that makes
it unique, modern and future-proof.

## Event Details

- **Date:** October 11, 2024
- **Location:** Milan, Italy
- **Venue:** Hotel Magna Pars

For more information and tickets, visit the official [MagentiamoDay 2024 website](https://www.magentiamo.it/magentiamo-day-2024/).

*Note: Event details are subject to change. Please check the official website for the most up-to-date information.*
