# Maho at DevConf 2026!

![DevConf 2026](/assets/blog/devconf-italia-2026-hero.png){ loading=lazy }

We're excited to share that **Fabrizio Balliano** will be speaking at **DevConf 2026**, the Italian conference dedicated to developers and the open source community.

<!-- more -->

DevConf is two days of talks, live coding, project showcases, and networking, all built around open source and the people who make it. It's exactly the kind of crowd Maho was born for: developers who care about owning their stack, building in the open, and keeping the web independent.

In his talk, **"Maho: la fenice delle piattaforme ecommerce, grazie all'open source"** (Maho: the phoenix of e-commerce platforms, thanks to open source), Fabrizio will be presenting Maho and its breakthrough history, tools and future.

## Event Details

- **Date:** July 7-8, 2026
- **Maho talk:** Tuesday, July 7, 2026, 15:25-15:55
- **Location:** Pavia, Italy
- **Venue:** Learning Space Cravino, Via Agostino Bassi 2

Admission is **free**, but seating is limited and requires advance reservation, so grab your spot early.

For the full schedule, talk lineup, and registration, visit the official [DevConf 2026 website](https://devconf.it/2026/){target=_blank}.

If you're coming, come say hi. We'd love to talk shop about open source e-commerce, on-premises freedom, and where Maho is headed next.

Maho rocks! 🚀
